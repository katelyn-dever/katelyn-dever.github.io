[meet tiffany]

Salon Owner, Tiffany Martin, started her career in 2005. It didn’t take long for her to have a following of clients that appreciate her attention to detail. Tiffany has always aspired to own a salon so that she could create the perfect experience. She feels that building a staff through apprenticeship is the best approach so that all of the stylists have the same core training and workmanship. She has trained under some of the nation’s best stylists and educators helping to make her one of the most sought after stylists in the Greenville area.

SERVICES

Not sure what you’re looking for? Here is a brief description of our services:

Balayage

A technique for highlighting hair in which lightener is painted on in such a way as to create a graduated, natural-looking effect.

Bleach Re-touch

Root touch up for solid blondes.

Blow Dry Coaching

Learn how to blow dry your hair at home with the help of your stylist.

Brazilian Bond Builder

Strengthening hair treatment for natural or color treated hair.

GK Smoothing Treatment

Not a straightening treatment. This treatment smooths frizz enhances shine and helps cut drying time by up to 50%. Lasts 3-5 months depending on at home care.

Color Retouch

Single color applied to your root.

Color Correction

Major shift in color (Example: Dark to light) , removing previous color or any transformation.

Glamour Strands

Hair tinsel attached to your natural hair strands. Lasts up to 1 week.

Gloss / Glaze

Can be clear to just add shine or have colored pigment to compliment, refresh or tone the hair.

Vivids

Fashion color application.

Pre-Color Treatment

Removal of chlorine, medications, styling products etc. Performed prior to certain color services to ensure results.

Day of Appointment

Please arrive on time to your appointment to ensure you receive your full service. If you are running behind, please contact the salon. We will try our best to accommodate you. If no communication within 15 minutes of being late please keep in mind that we may have to reschedule your appointment.

Bring a snack and your phone charger if you are scheduled for any service taking longer than one hour.
