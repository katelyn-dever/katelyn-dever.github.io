About Our Restaurant

The Farm


At Super Restaurant, the freshest ingredients are always our priority. For that reason, we farm 80% of our own ingredients. That's right, WE GROW THE FOOD. We own the farm, grow the produce, and pick it only when it's reached perfection. The other 20%? All locally grown, right here in Macon, GA. Nothing we serve to you will be anything less than the peak of freshness, we gaurantee it.

Our Team


Head chef and owner, Johnathan Bradley (the good-looking fellow in glasses), has 16 years of experience working in the food and beverage industry. Learning to cook from both parents, he grew up with a passion for food. Today he strives to share that passion with each and every Super Restaurant customer by bringing delicious, healthy, colorful, and fresh ingredients to the table every day. He and his wife, Samantha, are both from the Macon area and reside in their home built on the restaurant's farm with their daughters Astrid and Melanie.


Sous chef, Caleb Dupree, has 8 years of experience, all right here at Super Restaurant. He's been with us since the beginning and shares our passion for fresh, healthy food. Originally from Greenville, SC, he spent 3 years after finishing school traveling the world and sampling foods from as many countries as possible, enjoying every bite. Today, he loves implementing these diverse flavors into the dishes here at Super Restaurant.


Last, but definitely not least, in our team of highly qualified and passionate chefs, is station chef, Anna Ross. Her culinary vision helps bring everything together. From menu planning to plate styling, she handles every little detail in the kitchen, while keeping the boys in line, and making sure everything runs on time.
